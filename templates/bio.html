Let's Get To Know Each Other


Video Work

I am a video producer and editor based in New York City. After watching way too much television while growing up, I moved to New Orleans to study Digital Media Production at Tulane University. Once I got a taste of the area's burgeoning film industry, I decided to pursue my passion and explore a career as an editor. Post-college, I relocated to NYC to work in the booming startup scene, and have produced and edited video for companies including GoGoMix and One Month. My work has been featured in the New Orleans Uptown Sounds Film Fesival and the 2015 United Nations Enable Film Festival.
